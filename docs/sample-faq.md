## Return policy

Customers may return unopened products within 30 days of purchase for a full refund.
Opened products can be exchanged within 14 days if defective. Refunds are processed to
the original payment method within 5-7 business days.

## Shipping

Standard shipping takes 3-5 business days within the country. Express shipping
(1-2 business days) is available at checkout for an additional fee. International
shipping times vary by destination and are shown at checkout before payment.

## Support hours

Customer support is available Monday through Friday, 9:00-17:00 (local time). Support
requests submitted outside these hours are answered on the next business day.

## Account & billing

Subscriptions renew automatically unless canceled at least 24 hours before the renewal
date. Invoices are sent by email and are also available for download from the account
dashboard under "Billing history."

## Product warranty

All products carry a 12-month manufacturer warranty covering defects in materials and
workmanship. The warranty does not cover damage from misuse, unauthorized repairs, or
normal wear and tear. To file a warranty claim, contact support with your order number
and a description of the issue; approved claims are resolved by repair, replacement, or
refund at the company's discretion.

## International orders

International orders are subject to customs duties and import taxes determined by the
destination country, which are the customer's responsibility and are not included in
the checkout price. Delivery times for international orders range from 7 to 21 business
days depending on customs processing. Some products may be restricted from shipping to
certain countries due to local regulations.
